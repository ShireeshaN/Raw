ABOUT THE EVENT

RAW STRENGTH CALISTHENICS – HYDERABAD 2026 is an elite fitness showdown built for those who push beyond limits.

This event brings together passionate athletes, street workout performers, and fitness enthusiasts to compete in pure bodyweight challenges. From beginners to advanced competitors, every participant gets a platform to showcase their power and skill.

The competition is designed around three core pillars — strength, endurance, and control. Every movement is judged on form, technique, and consistency, making it a true test of discipline and performance.

It’s not just a competition — it’s a battle of raw strength, mindset, and mastery.
